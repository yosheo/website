Financial Reporting Dashboard with Power BI

The Problem: Field workers and finanical managers had no automated view of port traffic. Financial reports are generated on a monthly and quartely basis and was mostly a manual process.
The Solution: Build a Power BI Report that encompassed the Weekly, Quarterly, and Yearly timeseries. This was the most logical solution since the company was already using it in other fashions and many workers already had licenses. This was also the most cost effective, most easily distributable, and most easily accessible option.

Form App with Power Apps

The Problem: Communications systems' reports were generated every weekday to ensure the integrity of each resource's systems. The process was being performed manually each morning.
The Solution: Build a form app with Power Apps. The key user generates this report each morning by pulling the statuses of each resource from a few different systems. Data source integration wasn't possible to do within the client's timeframe. The solution then became to implement a form app that could take in communications statuses for each resource, save it to an Excel sheet hosted on the company's Sharepoint website, and integrate Power BI charts so other key users could better visualize this daily report.
